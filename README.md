## What is Hit the Lights?

Hit the Lights is a free-to-play rhythm game that runs entirely in your browser. Upload your favorite songs, play falling-note charts across multiple difficulties, and compete for high scores — no download required.

Built with a focus on tight timing, clean visuals, and social features, Hit the Lights is designed for both casual players and rhythm game veterans.

---

## Features

- **Lane-based rhythm gameplay** — four lanes with precise timing windows and responsive keyboard input
- **Multiple difficulties per song** — Easy to Expert charts, with custom chart upload support
- **Scoring & ranks** — Radiant, Shining, Glimmer, and Shattered ranks based on accuracy
- **Live HUD** — real-time accuracy percentage, combo counter, and max combo tracking
- **Custom controls & speed** — remap keys and adjust note fall speed (0.5x–2.5x)
- **6 UI themes** — Neon Gold, Neon Purple, Midnight Blue, Crimson Red, Toxic Green, and Light Mode
- **Song library** — upload songs with cover art, mark them public or private, browse the community library
- **Leaderboards** — per-difficulty global leaderboards and personal bests
- **Achievements** — Skill, Progression, and Social achievement categories with profile pinning
- **Social layer** — friend requests, in-game messaging, score sharing, song likes, and comments
- **Profiles** — custom profile pictures, playtime tracking, and analytics
- **Guest play** — play without an account; progress syncs when you register

## How to Play

1. Open **[hitthelights.xyz](https://hitthelights.xyz)** in any modern browser.
2. Register an account or play as a guest.
3. Browse public songs or upload your own track + cover art.
4. Pick a difficulty and hit the falling notes as they reach the hit zone.
5. Review your score, accuracy, and rank — then try to beat it.

**Default controls:** D · F · J · K  
*(Fully remappable in Settings)*

## Tech Stack

Hit the Lights is built on a modern full-stack architecture:

- **Frontend:** Angular 21, TypeScript, SCSS
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express
- **Database:** Neon (Serverless PostgreSQL)
- **Storage:** Cloudflare R2
- **Hosting:** Render
